Ishita & Pratik











We're getting married!

Ishita and Pratik are engaged to be married on January 22nd, 2015. Both the ceremony and reception will be held in Mumbai, India.

"Ishita is this fun-loving, crazy, adventurous girl, always up for a prank! After years of close friendship, silly pranks, stupid quests and stolen pineapples, we eventually fell in love :). Life suddenly feels like a symphony and every moment with her is a celebration. She completes me, makes me want to be a better person. From lab partners to life partners, we have come a long way..and we are only getting started. Love you Ishi!"

- Pratik

"I met Pratik for the first time in 2005, first year of undergraduate college. Little did I know that 10 years later, I would be getting married to him. Although we were friends for a long time, love bloomed much later. One day you look at a person and you see something more than you did the night before. Like a switch that has been flicked somewhere. And the person who was just a friend is.. suddenly the only person you can ever imagine yourself with. Love you!"

- Ishita
